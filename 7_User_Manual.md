# User Manual: Employee Location Tracking

Welcome to the **Employee Location Tracking System**. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for utilizing the platform's tracking and monitoring capabilities.

---

## 1. Field Staff (Employees)

### Reporting Your Current Location
1.  **Authentication**: Log in with your corporate credentials at the portal homepage.
2.  **Dashboard**: You will land on the simplified tracking interface.
3.  **GPS Permission**: Ensure your browser's location services are enabled. Click **"Allow"** when prompted.
4.  **Reporting**: Click the **"Record My Location"** button.
5.  **Confirmation**: A success notification will appear once your coordinates and address are securely logged.

---

## 2. Management & Administration

### Admin Command Center
1.  **Dashboard Overview**: View real-time activity feeds and total reporting stats for the day.
2.  **Live Map Terminal**: Navigate to the "Live Map" to visualize the current geographical distribution of the workforce.
    *   *Click markers to view individual staff names and reporting timestamps.*

### Workforce Management
- **Employee Directory**: Access the "Employees" tab to search, filter, and manage user profiles.
- **Track Logs**: Click the "History" icon on any employee profile to view their detailed location audit log.
- **Profile Updates**: Update mobile numbers, office assignments, or employment types as needed.

---

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

- **"Location access denied"**: Check your device settings and browser permissions for the portal.
- **"Unable to fetch coordinates"**: Ensure you have a stable data connection or GPS signal.
- **Login Issues**: If you forget your password, please contact the IT Administrator for a secure reset.
- **Map Accuracy**: The system relies on browser-provided GPS data; proximity to high-rising buildings or indoor environments may affect precision.
