# User Manual

Welcome to the Employee Location Tracking System. This guide helps you navigate the platform whether you are a staff member or an administrator.

---

## 1. For Employees

### Reporting Your Location
1.  **Log in**: Access the site using your registered email and password.
2.  **Home Page**: You will arrive at the Dashboard.
3.  **Permissions**: If prompted by your browser, click "Allow" to share your device's location.
4.  **Submit**: Click the **"Record My Location"** button.
5.  **Confirmation**: A success message will appear once your coordinates are safely stored.

### Account Management
- You can update your basic profile information if enabled by the administrator.

---

## 2. For Administrators

### Accessing the Admin Dashboard
1.  Log in with an account that has admin privileges.
2.  You will be automatically redirected to the **Admin Dashboard**.

### Viewing Employee Locations
- **Live Map**: Navigate to the "Live Map" section to see the latest reported locations of all staff members on an interactive map.
- **Recent Activity**: The dashboard lists the most recent submissions with timestamps and addresses.

### Managing Employees
1.  **Employee Directory**: Go to the "Employees" tab.
2.  **Search/Filter**: Find specific employees by name, ID number, or department.
3.  **Location History**: Click on an employee's name or the history icon to view their full track log over time.
4.  **Editing Profiles**: Click "Edit" to update an employee's mobile number, ID, or assigned office.

### System Configuration
- **User Roles**: Admins can promote regular users to admin status if needed.
- **Rate Limits**: The system restricts how often employees can submit locations to ensure performance.

---

## Troubleshooting
- **Location Not Found**: Ensure your browser's location services are enabled.
- **Login Failure**: Contact your system administrator for a password reset.
- **Map Not Loading**: Check your internet connection or verify map API configurations with the tech team.
