# User Manual: Preparedness, Safety & Continuity Portal: Workforce Locator

Welcome to the **Preparedness, Safety & Continuity Portal: Workforce Locator** portal. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for utilizing the platform's tracking, monitoring, and hazard response capabilities.

---

## 1. Field & Office Staff (Employees)

### Reporting Your Current Location
1.  **Authentication**: Log in with your corporate credentials at the portal homepage.
2.  **Dashboard**: You will see a simplified interface for check-ins.
3.  **Location Type**: Select whether you are reporting from **"Home"** or **"Office"**.
4.  **GPS Permission**: Click **"Allow"** when your browser asks for location access.
5.  **Recording**: Click the **"Record My Location"** button.
6.  **Confirmation**: A success notification will appear once your coordinates and address are securely logged.

---

## 2. Management & Administration

### Unified Command Center
1.  **Main Dashboard**: View real-time workforce statistics, total active personnel, and live disaster alerts (Earthquakes, NASA events).
2.  **Live Map Terminal**: Navigate to the integrated map to visualize the geographical distribution of the workforce.
    *   **Interaction**: Click employee markers to view details or disaster markers to see hazard intensity and metadata.
    *   **Layers**: Use the layer control to toggle between Active Faults, Volcano Locations, and KMZ environmental data.
    *   **Filtering**: Use the sidebar to filter employees by department, type, or specific office.

### Workforce Analysis
- **Workforce Geography**: A dedicated analysis page for comparing Home vs. Office distribution with synchronized list-map interactions.
- **Track Logs**: Access the "History" audit logs for any employee to review their movement patterns and address history.
- **Profile Management**: Update employee details, mobile numbers, and organizational assignments.

---

## Troubleshooting & FAQ

- **"Location access denied"**: Check your browser and device privacy settings; ensure the site has permission to access GPS.
- **"Map loading stuck"**: This may happen during heavy data synchronization. Refresh the page or check your internet connection.
- **Stale Disaster Data**: The system fetches live data periodically. If a known event isn't showing, wait a few minutes for the next sync cycle.
- **Accuracy**: GPS precision can be affected by indoor environments or high-density urban areas.
